Why Fishkill? It's an hour from the venue!

Ya, it's a little unforunate, here's what's going on:
  • There are no large hotels close to the venue. The closest is about 37 minutes away (Great Barrington, Massachusetts) but that's in the opposite direction since most people will be driving up from NJ/NY and the hotels are over $200/night.
  • There are some large hotels a little closer (~45 minutes) but many of them are: booked up, expensive ($250-300/night) and/or and or requiring two+ night stays. In Poughkeepsie, Marist College graduation is going on that weekend and farther north, there is a huge annual horse show in the area.
